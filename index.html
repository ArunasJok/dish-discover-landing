Dish Discover

AI Powered Web App for Sustainable Cooking

Dish Discover is a responsive web application designed to simplify meal planning by suggesting recipes based on the ingredients available at home. Leveraging the Spoonacular API and AI-powered recommendations, the app helps reduce food waste and promotes sustainable cooking practices. It features a robust CI/CD pipeline using Azure DevOps for seamless deployment.

Key Features

AI-Powered Recommendations

Get intelligent recipe suggestions based on your available ingredients and dietary preferences.

Sustainable Cooking

Reduce food waste by using what you already have in your kitchen with our smart recipe finder.

Responsive Design

Enjoy a seamless experience across all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile.
