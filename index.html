Dish Discover

AI Powered Web App for Sustainable Cooking

Dish Discover is a web-based app designed to help people decide what to cook based on the ingredients they already have at home. Instead of searching through cookbooks or random recipe sites, users can simply enter whatâ€™s in their fridge or cupboards, and the app will suggest recipes they can make. The app gets recipes from Spoonacular API. According to the Spoonacular API documentation, the platform provides comprehensive data on recipes, nutritional values, and ingredients, which enhances user experience in culinary applications (1). Also uses AI-powered recommendations to personalize suggestions over time. The goal is to make meal planning easier, reduce food waste, and help users make the most of what they already have.
